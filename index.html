What's happening here?

This page makes a cross-origin request with credentials to whatever URL you specify. If the target has a misconfigured CORS policy (like reflecting any Origin with Access-Control-Allow-Credentials: true), the browser will happily hand over the response — cookies and all.

Try it: ?target=https://vulnerable-api.example/me
